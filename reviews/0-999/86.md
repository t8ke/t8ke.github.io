
[**Review #86 | Scotch Review #33 - Bruichladdich Rocks**]( https://t8ke.review/review-86-bruichladdich-rocks/)

**The Schtick:** This is a red wine finished unpeated expression from Bruichladdich. Non chill filtered and natural color.

-----

**Distiller:** Bruichladdich

**Bottler:** Bruichladdich

**Selection:** N/A

**ABV:**  46% ABV

**Age:** NAS 

-----

**Nose:**  Honey, brine, some light floral fruit and some sweet cream. Pretty lactic, as I find most Bruichladdich spirit to be.     

**Taste:** Thin mouth feel but light, briney and sweet. Theres fruit and a hint of wine followed by more hay, honey and brine. 

**Finish:** Medium, more salty grain, followed by one last round of wine sweetness. 

**Overall:** Personally, I enjoy this dram. Its not as wine-forward as I was hoping but all in all, I think its solid. Its kind of a niche whiskey though and I cant say that I come home everyday and think to myself, "Rocks is the whiskey for tonight" but when I"m in the mood, its enjoyable.

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 6**
