
[**Review #110 | Scotch Review #42 - Bunnahabhain 10yr Signatory Vintage 2005**]( https://t8ke.review/review-110-bunnahabhain-signatory-vintage-2005-10-year/)

**The Schtick:** This is a 10 year IB from Bunnahabhain that is bottled at cask strength and matured full term in a first fill sherry butt. 

-----

**Distiller:** Bunnahabhain

**Bottler:** Signatory Vintage

**Selection:** Binnys

**ABV:** 57.8% ABV

**Age:** NAS 

-----

**Nose:**  Sweet sherry and the maritime air. Molasses, Turkish delight and figs. Grapes, raisins, dates and moderate peat.

**Taste:** Sweet sherry, fruity and nutty with lots of salted toffee.

**Finish:** Long and deeply complex. Everything from the nose and palate carry into the finish and just stick around.  

**Overall:** Im beyond impressed and this is an easy favorite for me from the three. Theres just so much going on in this dram. Id buy again in a heartbeat.  

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 8**
