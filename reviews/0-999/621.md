
[**Review #621 | Scotch Review #209 - Glenrothes 17yr Signatory Vintage Unchillfiltered Collection**]( https://t8ke.review/review-621-glenrothes-17yr-signatory-ucf/)

**The Schtick:** This is an IB from Signatory capitalizing on Glenrothes spirit. It is matured in a refill sherry butt and bottled at 46% ABV. It is not colored or filtered.  

-----

**Distiller:** Glenrothes

**Bottler:** Signatory Vintage

**Selection:** N/A

**ABV:**  46% ABV

**Age:** 17 Years 

-----

**Nose:**  Sweet honey. Nutty sherry. Leather, tobacco, vanilla and butter cream. Faint stewed fruits. Faint sulfur. 

**Taste:** Thin mouthfeel, faint sulfur. NO real heat. Sweet melon, faint sherry notes that are reminiscent of PX. Vanilla and butter cream are pretty prominent with a clover honey note. 

**Finish:** Short, mildly astringent. Growing sulfur character. Some sweet cherry notes and then its gone. 

**Overall:** I think the nose was the best part of this - super complex and inviting. Unfortunately, the thin character of the palate and the short finish really keep it from climbing higher than a 5. 

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 5**
