
[**Review #308 | Scotch Review #128 - Tobermory 18yo Old Particular**]( https://t8ke.review/review-308-highland-park-dark-origins/)

**The Schtick:** 

-----

**Distiller:** Tobermory

**Bottler:** Douglas Laing

**Selection:** KLWines

**ABV:** 48.4% ABV

**Age:** NAS 

-----

**Nose:**  RIch sherry, honey, molasses, toffee, figs, raspberry jam, strawberries

**Taste:** Thin mouthfeel but sweet and definitely sherried. Toffee, vanilla, strawberry and raspberry jam. NO sulfur. Peat sneaks in and grows over time. Nice mild ashy peat

**Finish:** More mild smoke here, growing a little bitter. Faint fruit and some mild sweet and pepper

**Overall:** Its fine - nothing too wildly impressive or complex. Fruity, smokey then sweet. I cant help but wish that this was closer to cask strength and maybe that would have pushed this from forgettable and plain, but still good, to a great whiskey. Im giving this a Rank 5 as a result.

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 5**
