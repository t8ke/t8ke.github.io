
[**Review #629 | Scotch Review #213 - Ardbeg Kelpie Committee Release**]( https://t8ke.review/review-629-ardbeg-kelpie-cr/)

**The Schtick:** This is the higher proof release of Ardbegs Kelpie bottling. 

-----

**Distiller:** Ardbeg

**Bottler:** Ardbeg

**Selection:** N/A

**ABV:** 51.7% ABV

**Age:** NAS 

-----

**Nose:**   Floral and sweet. Lots of vanilla, confectioners sugar and faint peat pairing with oceanic salinity. 

**Taste:** Lime and honey. Really light and floral - much more brine on the palate than the nose. Ashy telephone pole peat rolls in at the tail end. 

**Finish:** Medium length, faintly lactic milk, some astringency followed by a floral finish.  

**Overall:** This was nice but weirdly light - even with the elevated proof. The astringency on the finish wasnt really impressive, but the rest of the dram was solid. 

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 5**
