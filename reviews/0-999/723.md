
[**Review #723 | Bourbon Review #245 - Caol Ila 20yr AD Rattray Bottling**]( https://t8ke.review/review-723-caol-ila-20yr-ad-rattray-selection/)

**The Schtick:** This is a 20 year old, cask strength, ex-bourbon matured bottling from A.D Rattray featuring Caol Ila distillate. It was sent to me as a mystery sample and I have filled in the relevant info post reveal after tasting live in the Whiskey Network Discord and getting the reveal there. 

-----

**Distiller:** Caol Ila

**Bottler:** A.D. Rattray

**Selection:** N/A

**ABV:** 50.2% ABV

**Age:** 20 Years 

-----

**Nose:**  Brash peat, lemon lime, bbq, meaty. sweet buttercream.

**Taste:** Thick mouthfeel, oily. Heat is apparent but not punishing, lime returns with strong oak presence. Vanilla butter cream, faint stewed fruits, cocoa, toffee.

**Finish:** Medium, but crisp. drying, sweet cocoa, toffee, vanilla, ashy peat and a notable lack of heat here

**Overall:** 

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 7**
