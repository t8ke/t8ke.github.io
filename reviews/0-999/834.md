
[**Review #834 | Scotch Review #281 - Douglas Laings: The Epicurean**]( https://t8ke.review/review-834-douglas-laings-the-epicurean/)

**The Schtick:** This is a Douglas Laing blended release made from a variety of lowland expressions. 

-----

**Distiller:** Various

**Bottler:** Douglas Laing

**Selection:** N/A

**ABV:** 46.2% ABV

**Age:** NAS 

-----

**Nose:**   Honey, peaches, pears, vanilla, waxy fruits. 

**Taste:** Thin, mild heat, vanilla, pears, pepper, waxy fruits, mild smoke. Reminds me  of a weaker Great Kings Street from Compass Box, almost. 

**Finish:** Short, thin, hot, fruity pears. Faint lime, waxy fruits. 

**Overall:** Not bad but too hot in the finish for what it is. Other than the heat, this is really enjoyable, mustache and all. 

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 4**
