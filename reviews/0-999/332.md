**Nose:**  honey, jam, raspberry and jelly. marmalade, strawberry, pepper, mild peat

**Taste:** sweet syrupy jam and peat. vanilla, pound cake, pepper, good mouthfeel

**Finish:** Long, good heat, sweet, peppery, fruity peat. Much more integrated. Mild astringency

**Overall:** This is a really great port influenced peated dram. I think I still prefer Laphroaig Portwood but this is easily found and well priced where it sits so Im not complaining. Im going to give this a Rank 7 - delicious and complex.
