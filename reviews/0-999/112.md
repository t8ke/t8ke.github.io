
[**Review #112 | Scotch Review #43 - Bruichladdich Waves**]( https://t8ke.review/review-112-bruichladdich-waves/)

**The Schtick:** Another funky one off from Bruichladdich in their Elements series (Rocks, Waves, etc)

-----

**Distiller:** Bruichladdich

**Bottler:** Bruichladdich

**Selection:** N/A

**ABV:**  46% ABV

**Age:** NAS 

-----

**Nose:**  Smoke and vanilla, custard and meringue, lemon and heavy cream. Really interesting nose and I like how the smoke and sweet mingle among one another

**Taste:** Sweet, creamy vanilla. Fruit snacks. Lemon pie. Smoke kicks in swiftly with a healthy dose of laddie brine. Icing follows. Really funky balance of dessert and smoke. 

**Finish:** Medium and smokey. The smoke really settles in here and the classic Bruichladdich bbq bacon notes strike as well. Theres some sweet here as well but its mostly a smoke fest. 

**Overall:** This is a really unique dram and I like it a lot. This is one expression I wish had not gone away. The nose is all smoke and the palate is all sweets and fruit and it continues to keep me guessing.  

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 7**
