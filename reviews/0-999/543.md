**Bottler:** Compass Box

**Selection:** N/A

**ABV:**  46% ABV

**Age:** NAS 

-----

**Nose:**   Apples, honey, vanilla, buttercream, not wildly complex and reminds me of a number of products of theirs. 

**Taste:** Medium mouthfeel, apples, honey, faint pepper and smoke. Vanilla, caramel are big

**Finish:** Medium, some heat, very apple forward, paired with apricot, pepper and the faintest smoke whisp

**Overall:** Good but not exceptional. Unfortunately a descriptor Im using more and more often for CB releases
