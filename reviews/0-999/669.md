
[**Review #669 | Bourbon Review #386 - Blantons Straight from the Barrel 1/8/2015**]( https://t8ke.review/review-669-blantons-straight-from-the-barrel-dump-1-8-2015/)

**The Schtick:** This is a Mashbill #2 release from Buffalo Trace that is international only. It is bottled at cask strength.

-----

**Distiller:** Buffalo Trace

**Bottler:** Buffalo Trace

**Selection:** N/A

**ABV:** 65.35% ABV

**Age:** NAS 

-----

**Nose:**  Big vanilla, earthy pepper. Toffee, oak, sour fruit, faint ethanol.

**Taste:** Medium mouthfeel, rich honey, a good deal of heat. Earthy pepper, bitter cocoa, faint vanilla. The heat is pretty prominent here but the earthy cocoa is interesting. 

**Finish:** Long and hot, but loaded with vanilla and a bit of earthy cocoa. 

**Overall:** These are always really different to my palate, and I cant say I love them, but the palate on this SFTB was interesting and unique.  

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 5**
