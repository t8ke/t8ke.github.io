
[**Review #803 | Bourbon Review #440 - Blantons Gold**]( https://t8ke.review/review-804-blantons-gold)

**The Schtick:** This is an international only release of Blantons, a popular single barrel bourbon released by Buffalo Trace in the USA. The major selling point behind Blantons Gold, apart from its exclusivity of markets, is that it is bottled at a higher proof than regular Blantons. Lets see how it is.  

-----

**Distiller:** Buffalo Trace

**Bottler:** Buffalo Trace

**Selection:** N/A

**ABV:** 51.5% ABV

**Age:** NAS 

-----

**Nose:**  Honey, caramel, clove and tobacco. Some corn syrup, earthy pepper. 

**Taste:** Medium mouth feel, clove and brown sugar. Maple, honey, oak. Mild fruits, earthy tobacco.

**Finish:** Medium length, earthy pepper, nice cocoa and creamy wafer. Some tobacco.

**Overall:** Not too bad - I like this better than the OG Blantons and about the same as some of their Straight From The Barrel expressions. 

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 6**
