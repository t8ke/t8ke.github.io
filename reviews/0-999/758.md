**The Schtick:** This is a sample of Old Weller Antique that I received in a blind sample. I got the reveal after taking down notes and am using that to fill in information here for convenience. For other blind reviews, hop on over to the  Whiskey Network Discord server and join the chatter. 

-----

**Distiller:** Buffalo Trace

**Bottler:** Buffalo Trace

**Selection:** Bourbon Brigante

**ABV:** 53.5% ABV

**Age:** NAS 

-----

**Nose:**  Definitely bourbon. Ethanol, clove, caramel, vanilla. Tobacco, fruity with mild passion fruit and cherry.   

**Taste:** Rich and sweet. No heat. Caramel, toffee and a nice thick mouth feel. Clove, mild chalkiness, fruity orange and passion fruit. 

**Finish:** Medium, crisp and clean with no heat. Sweetness carries well.
