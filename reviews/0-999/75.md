
[**Review #75 | Scotch Review #28 - Caol Ila 12**]( https://t8ke.review/review-75-caol-ila-12yy/)

**The Schtick:** This is Caol Ilas 12yr old expression from their core line of age stated products. 

-----

**Distiller:** Caol Ila

**Bottler:** Caol Ila

**Selection:** N/A

**ABV:**  43% ABV

**Age:** 12 Years 

-----

**Nose:**  Smokey with mild vegetation. Lots of sweet honey and mild brine.     

**Taste:** Theres a lot of smoke followed by custard sweetness. Im amazed at how much salty caramel follows. For being smokey, its a really sweet dram to me and I love it. Theres a tiny bit of vegetable notes, but not in an off putting way. This salty, smokey, sweet profile is really starting to become a favorite of mine.  

**Finish:** Medium to long. Sweet and smokey, taking turns having their moment. Gentle, but it is fairly low proof. 

**Overall:** This is a very solid pour with great balance. 

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 6**
