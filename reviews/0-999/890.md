
[**Review #890 | Scotch Review #299 - Ledaig 10yr**]( https://t8ke.review/review-890-ledaig-10/)

**The Schtick:** This is a core expression release from Ledaig, which is peated distillate hailing from Tobermory distillery. 

-----

**Distiller:** Tobermory

**Bottler:** Tobermory

**Selection:** N/A

**ABV:**  43% ABV

**Age:** 10 Years 

-----

**Nose:**  Huge mineral wiff, earthy, dirty peat. Faint iodine. Faint fishy marine notes. Vanilla cream, confectioners sugar. Nice brine and pepper component.   

**Taste:** Thin, but no heat. Sweet vanilla, lots of chalky, mineral peat smoke. Some pepper, caramel and cream. 

**Finish:** Long, earthy and smokey. Lots of minerals here. The peat smoke is more earthy, mineral and peppery than ashy or super fish like. 

**Overall:** This is really great, especially for a younger core expression. Nice work, Ledaig. 

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 6**
