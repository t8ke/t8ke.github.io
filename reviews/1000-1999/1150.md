**The Schtick:** This is a Knob Creek rye rebarreled in #4 charred barrels. 

-----

**Distiller:** Beam

**Bottler:** Beam

**Selection:** N/A

**ABV:**  50% ABV

**Age:** NAS 

-----

**Nose:**  Honey, spice, peanuts, floral and fresh. Pepper, vanilla, fresh baked bread. 

**Taste:** Thin mouth feel. No heat. Sweet caramel, musty cardboard, honey and confectioners sugar. Grassy and floral. Faint oak presence.

**Finish:** Dry, oaky and musty. Reminds me more of a craft finish than anything. Sweet honey, grassy musty and gross. 

**Overall:** More like a young craft rye than something notable from Beam. Dissappointing, to say the least.
