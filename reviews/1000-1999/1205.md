
[**Review #1205 | Scotch Review #411 - Staoisha 3yr Heavily Peated Bunna**]( https://t8ke.review/review-1205-stoisha-3yr-heavily-peated-bunna)

**The Schtick:** This was a bottle that I tried blind and then got the reveal in order to fill in relevant details here. 

-----

**Distiller:** Bunnahabhain

**Bottler:** Bunnahabhain

**Selection:** KLWines

**ABV:**  56% ABV

**Age:** 3 Years 

-----

**Nose:**  Sweet cream, pungent peat and moss. Earthy, oceanic, mossy. 

**Taste:** Medium to thick mouthfeel. Sweet cream, lemon, honey, ginger, almond, rich dead fish. Mossy, rich and smokey. Light ash. 

**Finish:** Long, sweet and rich with peat. Smokey, ashy, lots of fishy unami. 

**Overall:** Good and shockingly brash / complex for the age. A tiny bit hot, but the complexity here is a winner despite its youth. 

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 6**
