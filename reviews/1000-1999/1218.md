
[**Review #1218 | Rum Review #78 - Hampden HGML Unaged**]( https://t8ke.review/review-1218-hampden-hgml-unaged)

**The Schtick:** This is an unaged high ester rum distilled at Hampden distillery and held by E&A Scheer. It carries the HGML marque.

-----

**Distiller:** Hampden

**Bottler:** Fine Drams

**Selection:** Fine Drams

**ABV:**  69% ABV

**Age:** N/A 

-----

**Nose:**  Syrupy and rich in fruits. Pineapple, coconut, toffee. Lots going on, with an emphasis of fruits over searing funk. 

**Taste:** Thick mouth feel. Sweeter and less brash in the ester department compared to LROK and DOK, but certainly no slouch. Lots of pineapple, sweet syrup, coconut and toffee. Light pepper and ash. Hot. 

**Finish:** Long and rich with lots more rubber showing in the finish than the nose or palate. Syrupy pineapple. 

**Overall:** Really something delicious. Great evolution, incredibly potent despite the unique coconut note I got a lot of hear. Love it. 

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 7**
