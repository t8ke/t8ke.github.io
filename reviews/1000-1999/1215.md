
[**Review #1215 | Rum Review #77 - Hampden LROK Unaged**]( https://t8ke.review/review-1215-hampden-lrok-unaged)

**The Schtick:** This is an unaged rum distilled at Hampden distillery and held by E&A Scheer. It carries the LROK marque.

-----

**Distiller:** Hampden

**Bottler:** Fine Drams

**Selection:** FineDrams

**ABV:**  69% ABV

**Age:** N/A 

-----

**Nose:**  Potent mango, ethanol, beef jerky, fruits. Bright and effervescent, exceptionally crisp.

**Taste:** Medium mouth feel, but at the cost of significant heat. Vanilla, honey, rubber and funk. Lightly rotting. Total fruit bomb. 

**Finish:** Long rich and loaded with mango, banana and essence of burning rubber.

**Overall:** Potent but incredibly clean. This was a total treat to try, especially given the searing funk and fruit balance. 

Thanks for reading!

**Rank: 6**
